Projects
Controller Card is a a capstone project. It is a controller that offers a simple PC interface from which to control and monitor circuits. It is completely open source (under GPL v3). It features USB to PC communication, ability to control and monitor 8 inputs and 8 outputs, as well as SPI broken out to allow for future expansion.
Proposal and Site

RAUPS is a fork and redesign of the hardware associated with RAMPS, a 3D printer controler shield. It stands for RepRap Arduino Uno Pololu Shield. This is an attempt to make the RepRap firmware available on a smaller, cheaper controller than the Due or Mega.
