Projects
Controller Card is a a capstone project. It is a PC to circuit interface, similar to solutions offered from companies like National Instruments. It is completely open source. It features USB to PC communication, ability to control and monitor 8 inputs and 8 outputs, as well as SPI broken out to allow for future expansion. It is currently in very early development.
Proposal and Site

RAUPS is a fork and redesign of the hardware associated with RAMPS, a 3D printer controler shield. It stands for RepRap Arduino Uno Pololu Shield, and is currently in the testing and debugging stages of development. This is an attempt to make the RepRap firmware available on a smaller, cheaper controller than the Due or Mega. This project is almost out of the development stage and in the support stage.
